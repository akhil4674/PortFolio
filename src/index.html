FZI Research Center for Information Technology

During my time at FZI Forschungszentrum Informatik, I was involved in exciting research projects focused on developing and refining advanced robotic systems. I delved into the world of robotics simulation, working on ROS packages for the Boston Dynamics Spot robot. By integrating high-fidelity physics engines, I created realistic and immersive testing environments that enabled us to push the boundaries of robot behavior and performance. Furthermore, I explored the integration of machine learning algorithms to enhance robot behavior prediction and decision-making. This allowed us to develop more intelligent and adaptable robots capable of navigating complex scenarios and executing tasks with precision.
